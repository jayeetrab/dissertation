# Multi-City Task Set — Review Sheet

80 tasks (8 per city). Please check: (1) policy codes are correct for each city, (2) the named district makes sense, (3) task wording is realistic planning practice. Codes were verified to exist in each plan's text; titles were not always extractable, so a planner's eye is needed.


## Manchester

**MAN-PC-01** (policy_compliance, medium)
> Does a proposed 80-unit residential development on a brownfield site in Manchester city centre comply with the housing policies of the Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027 and the NPPF December 2024? Identify which specific policies and paragraphs apply.
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy H1, Policy H8, Policy H11, NPPF para 11, NPPF para 120

**MAN-PC-02** (policy_compliance, medium)
> A developer proposes a major residential scheme in Manchester with no affordable housing contribution, citing viability. What does the Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027 require for affordable housing, and under what circumstances can the requirement be reduced? Cite the specific policy.
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy H8

**MAN-ES-01** (evidence_synthesis, medium)
> Summarise the overall spatial strategy and housing requirement set out in the Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027, citing the key strategic policies (Policy H1, Policy H8, Policy H11 where relevant).
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy H1, Policy H8, Policy H11

**MAN-ES-02** (evidence_synthesis, hard)
> Compare the climate change and sustainable design requirements of the Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027 (e.g. Policy EN4) with the NPPF December 2024. Where does the local plan, adopted in 2012, fall short of or differ from current national policy?
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy EN4

**MAN-SA-01** (site_appraisal, hard)
> Assess the suitability of a hypothetical 2-hectare former industrial site in Ancoats, Manchester, for a residential-led mixed-use development. Identify the relevant policies from the Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027 and any likely constraints.
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy H1, Policy H8, Policy H11, Policy EN1

**MAN-SC-01** (stakeholder_communication, easy)
> Write a plain-English summary for local residents explaining what Policy EN1 of the Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027 means for the design of new development in their neighbourhood.
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy EN1

**MAN-ST-01** (strategic_analysis, hard)
> The Manchester Core Strategy 2012-2027 was adopted in 2012, before the NPPF December 2024 revision. Identify the policy areas where the December 2024 changes (grey belt, mandatory housing targets, affordable housing amendments) supersede or create tension with the adopted plan.
- expected: NPPF_December_2024.pdf — NPPF Annex 1

**MAN-ST-02** (strategic_analysis, medium)
> What affordable housing percentage and site-size threshold applies to major residential developments in Manchester, and which local plan policy sets this? Answer for Manchester specifically.
- expected: Manchester_Core_Strategy_2012.pdf — Policy H8
